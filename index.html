Plot

The setting of the movie is in Silicon Valley over Christmas, Ned (Bryan Cranston), his wife and son go to meet their college daughter's millionaire boyfriend Laird (James Franco) and he is not at all what they expected. He is extremely vulgar and open with his feelings and activities. The comedy in this movie is very vulgar and not recommended for children. Ned does not approve of Laird the entire movie, but later on in the movie have to reconcil for his daughter after a crazy holiday. Ned ends up approving Laird's request to marry his daughter in the end.
